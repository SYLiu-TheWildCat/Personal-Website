Summer 2023:

Linear Algebraic Groups

Description: This is an online example-based seminar in summer 2023 for linear algebraic groups. The aim of this seminar is to understand representation and structure theory of affine linear $k$-groups. The main reference of this seminar is the first two parts of [MT11].
