Spring 2024:
Nadler-Zaslow Correspondence at a Point

Introduction: This seminar is devoted to understand (wrapped) Floer theory and microlocal theory of sheaves on Weinstein sectors and their identification. It's a "localized" version(although not rigorously defined) of the Nadler-Zaslow correspondence in [NZ09]. The main references of this seminar will be the series of papers [GPS1, GPS2, GPS3], as well as some subsequent developments, for example [Kuo23].
